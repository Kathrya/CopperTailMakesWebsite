About CopperTail Makes

CopperTail Makes is a creative shop offering small 3D printed designs, crafted with care and inspired by the playful spirit of a fox. From copper-toned pendants to low-poly fox figures and unique desk accessories, every piece is made in-house with attention to detail. Items are printed in PLA filament and finished by hand, ready to ship or made to order.
